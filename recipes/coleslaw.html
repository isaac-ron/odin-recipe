Traditional Creamy Coleslaw Recipe

Description

A traditional coleslaw recipe that's easy to make with creamy mayonnaise, horseradish dressing, and a bag of ready-made coleslaw mix.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • &1frasl4; white sugar
  • 2 tbsp. seasoned rice wine vinegar
  • 1 &1frasl2; tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp. prepared horseradish
  • &1frasl2; tsp. onion powder
  • &1frasl2; tsp. dry mustard
  • &1frasl2; tsp. celery salt
  • &1frasl2; tsp salt
  • &1frasl2; tsp. ground black pepper
  • 1 (28oz.) package coleslaw mix

Steps

  • Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, lemon juice, horseradish, onion powder, dry mustard, celery salt, salt, and pepper in a large mixing bowl until sugar has dissolved.
  • Fold coleslaw mix into dressing until well combined. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
